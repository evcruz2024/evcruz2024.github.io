Satirical Essay

What are Satirical Essays? It is writing is a style of writing that uses satire to criticize or poke fun at a subject. A satirical writer often uses such devices as hyperbole and irony to get his point across. Satirical essays are often aimed at political candidates, celebrities or situations that are absurd.
Here are some examples of satirical essays:

  • A Modest Proposal by Jonathan Swift
  • The Rainbow Horizon by Karen S. Cole
  • The Awful German Language by Mark Twain
  • Taming the Bicylcle also by Mark Twain

What is CSS? CSS stands for Cascading Style Sheets with an emphasis placed on “Style.” While HTML is used to structure a web document (defining things like headlines and paragraphs, and allowing you to embed images, video, and other media), CSS comes through and specifies your document’s style—page layouts, colors, and fonts are all determined with CSS.
